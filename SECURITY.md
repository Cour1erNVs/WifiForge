## Reporting a Vulnerability

1. Create a new issue bt navigating to "Issues" and clicking "New Issue".
2. Click "Bug Report".
3. Set the Title To "Bug | INSERT BRIEF DESCIPTION"
4. Click "Create".
5. Give the team up to 10 business days to fix the issue.
